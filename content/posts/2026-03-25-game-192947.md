In a world where productivity and discipline often take center stage, Zac Clark, the former Bachelorette star and addiction recovery advocate, reminds us that its okay to indulge in simple pleasures. His late-night guilty pleasure, a free puzzle game called Blocky Blast, highlights the importance of balance in our lives, even for someone who leads a structured existence.

During a recent interview, Clark shared how he unwinds after long days filled with marathon training and running his behavioral healthcare organization, Release Recovery. While he typically adheres to a routine that includes meditation and reflective writing, he humorously admitted that sometimes, the allure of a fun puzzle game takes precedence over his nightly rituals. This candidness not only makes him relatable but also emphasizes the value of authenticity in our daily lives.

As we navigate our own busy schedules, its essential to carve out time for relaxation and enjoyment, even if it means playing a game instead of sticking to our ideal routines. How do you find balance in your life amidst the demands of work and personal commitments?
