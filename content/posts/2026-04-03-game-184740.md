The excitement is palpable as beXide announces the full release of *Fruit Mountain Party*, their vibrant anime-styled 3D puzzle game, set to launch on April 23rd. After a successful early access period, the developers have taken player feedback to heart, enhancing the gameplay experience and stability. This game promises to deliver chaotic fun for both solo players and friends alike, with its unique fruit-merging mechanics and competitive modes.

*Fruit Mountain Party* transforms the beloved merging gameplay into a lively party battle format, allowing up to four players to compete locally or online. With various game modes like Standard Race and Thief Frenzy, players can enjoy a diverse gaming experience. The addition of features for the Nintendo Switch 2, such as improved frame rates and GameShare capabilities, further elevates the excitement.
