Microsoft has just unveiled an exciting lineup of 13 new titles coming to Xbox Game Pass, including the highly anticipated Forza Horizon 6, set to launch on May 19. With titles like Mixtape and Subnautica 2 also on the list, players have a lot to look forward to this month.

The full lineup showcases a diverse range of games, catering to different tastes and preferences. From action-packed adventures to immersive simulations, theres something for everyone.
