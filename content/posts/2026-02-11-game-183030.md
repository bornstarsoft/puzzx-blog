Apple has just announced an exciting lineup of new games coming to Apple Arcade, with the highlight being "Oceanhorn 3: Legend of the Shadow Sea." Launching on March 5, 2026, this action-RPG-adventure game invites players to explore a vibrant world nearly a thousand years after the events of its predecessor. With stunning 3D graphics and cross-device compatibility, it promises to be a captivating experience for both fans and newcomers alike.

In addition to Oceanhorn 3, Apple Arcade is set to welcome three more titles: "Pocket Love!+", a charming life simulator; "Flow Free+", a challenging puzzle game; and "Doraemon Dorayaki Shop Story+", where players can manage a sweetshop alongside the beloved manga character. These titles will also be available on March 5, expanding the already impressive library of games on the platform.

As Apple Arcade continues to evolve, it raises the question: what other surprises does Apple have in store for gamers in the future? Stay tuned for more updates and enjoy the adventures that await!
