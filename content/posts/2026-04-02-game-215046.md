In an exciting twist for mobile gamers, two iconic franchises, Tetris and Space Invaders, have joined forces for their first-ever collaboration. This partnership, launched by Playstudios and Taito, introduces a unique mode in the Tetris mobile app where players can engage in a series of special events, including a daily login challenge and a tournament that promises exclusive rewards. This collaboration not only enhances gameplay but also celebrates the legacy of both games in a fresh and engaging way.

The collaboration kicks off with the "Days of Invaders" event, running from April 1-12, where players can collect stickers to unlock exclusive cosmetics. Following this, from April 13-23, players can participate in Earth Protection Events in partnership with Dots.Eco, where completing levels contributes to real-world wildlife conservation efforts. The culmination of this event will be the "Invaders Showdown Tournament" from April 24-30, where players can compete for top rankings and special prizes.

This innovative crossover not only brings nostalgia for long-time fans but also introduces new players to the beloved mechanics of Tetris while engaging them in meaningful activities.
