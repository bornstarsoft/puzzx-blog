If you're a fan of cozy games and cats, then the upcoming release of Kitty Haven is sure to catch your attention. This delightful puzzle game invites players to create the perfect sanctuary for their feline friends, each with their own unique personalities and needs. Whether you're dealing with picky eaters or feisty furballs, the challenge lies in arranging the space to ensure harmony among your adorable residents.

Kitty Haven not only allows you to unlock various items and new residents but also encourages you to solve engaging puzzles that will help you decorate and personalize your haven. With its charming graphics and soothing gameplay, this game promises to be a delightful escape for cat lovers and puzzle enthusiasts alike. The demo is currently available on Steam, giving you a taste of whats to come.
