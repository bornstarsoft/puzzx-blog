He hinted that the gameplay will offer a unique experience that can only be realized on Nintendo hardware, suggesting an innovative twist that could redefine horror gaming.

While details remain scarce, Babieno's excitement is palpable. He mentioned that Project M introduces a bold concept that no other horror game has explored before, which raises the anticipation for what this game might entail. As a long-time Nintendo fan, Babieno's vision for Project M is not just about creating a decent game; it's about crafting an unforgettable experience that pays homage to the classics while pushing boundaries.
