Game Pass has just added a gem to its library: the critically acclaimed prison survival RPG, Back to the Dawn. With an overwhelmingly positive rating on Steam, this pixel art game invites players to step into the shoes of Thomas the Fox, an investigative journalist wrongfully imprisoned for uncovering government secrets. The games compelling narrative and engaging gameplay have already earned it multiple award nominations, including Best Game at the 2024 BIG Festival.

Launched alongside its 1.0 version on Steam, Back to the Dawn is available on Game Pass for PC, Xbox, and Cloud, making it accessible to a wide audience. For those who prefer to own it outright, its available on Xbox Series X|S for $19.99, with a limited-time launch discount bringing it down to $16.99 for the first week. With other exciting titles like RoboCop: Rogue City and Grounded 2 also releasing in July, Game Pass continues to offer an impressive lineup for gamers.

As we look ahead, it will be interesting to see how Back to the Dawn evolves within the gaming community. Will it become a staple in the RPG genre? Only time will tell.
