In a significant move for the gaming industry, SHIFT UP has announced its acquisition of 100% of Japanese game developer Unbound Inc. This strategic decision not only internalizes development staff but also enhances SHIFT UP's pipeline for PC and console games, positioning the company for future growth in a competitive market.

Unbound, led by the renowned CEO Shinji Mikamifamous for his work on iconic titles such as the Biohazard series and Devil May Crybrings a wealth of experience and talent to SHIFT UP. This acquisition is expected to bolster SHIFT UP's capabilities in creating high-quality gaming experiences, potentially leading to innovative new titles that could captivate players worldwide.

As the gaming landscape continues to evolve, this acquisition raises questions about the future direction of SHIFT UP and its plans for leveraging Unbound's expertise. Will we see a new wave of groundbreaking games emerge from this collaboration? Only time will tell.
