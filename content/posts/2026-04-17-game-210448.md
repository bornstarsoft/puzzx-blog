In the world of indie gaming, few titles manage to blend emotional storytelling with engaging gameplay as effectively as "Save My Scrap." Set to launch on April 22, 2026, this visual novel and puzzle game invites players to repair a broken android, Harima, while unraveling a poignant narrative that explores themes of memory and connection. The game is a significant upgrade from its predecessor, "Fix My Junk," and promises a more immersive experience with new dialogues, endings, and a gallery mode.

Players will step into the shoes of a mechanic tasked with restoring Harima, an aging android whose past is intertwined with that of his owner, Dr. Miomaru. As you navigate the mechanics of repair, youll also delve into the emotional depths of the characters, making choices that will lead to multiple endings. This unique blend of gameplay and storytelling is what sets "Save My Scrap" apart in a crowded market.

With its stunning cyberpunk art style and multilingual support, the game is poised to resonate with a global audience. As we await its release, one cant help but wonder how this game will redefine the boundaries of narrative-driven gameplay in the indie scene. Will you be ready to uncover the truths hidden within the circuits of Harima?
