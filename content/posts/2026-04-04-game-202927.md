Get ready for an exciting new addition to the gaming world! On April 8, the physics-based urban survival game "Good Luck" will make its debut on PC, bringing a unique blend of platforming and cooperative gameplay. Players will navigate a city filled with absurd traps and unusual hazards, all while trying to reach a meeting point. With both single-player and online co-op modes for up to five players, this game promises to deliver a thrilling experience.

Developed by the independent Turkish studio Happybara Games and published by Ultimate Games S.A., "Good Luck" is set to expand its reach to PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S consoles in the future. This game not only showcases the creativity of indie developers but also highlights the growing trend of cooperative gaming experiences that bring friends together in fun and challenging environments.

As we anticipate the launch, one has to wonder: will "Good Luck" redefine urban survival games and set a new standard for multiplayer experiences? Stay tuned for more updates!
