If youre looking for a delightful blend of action and puzzle-solving, look no further than nDreams latest release, Oh My Galaxy! This charming arcade game invites players to defend planets from invading asteroids by flinging their own projectiles in a vibrant cosmic setting. With its engaging mechanics and stunning visuals, its a must-play for any VR enthusiast.

Available on Meta Quest platforms, Oh My Galaxy! offers both virtual reality and mixed reality modes, allowing players to immerse themselves in a beautifully crafted universe or bring the cosmic chaos into their living rooms. The games intuitive controls, whether using a controller or hand-tracking, make it accessible for players of all ages, ensuring that everyone can enjoy the thrill of cosmic destruction.

What truly sets Oh My Galaxy! apart is its simplicity and charm. The game strips away unnecessary complexity, focusing instead on a pure and enjoyable gameplay experience. As you progress through the levels, youll find yourself hooked on the challenge of mastering the mechanics while enjoying the delightful interactions with the personified celestial bodies. Will you take on the challenge and save the galaxy?
