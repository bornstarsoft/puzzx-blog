Phantom Feline, the charming puzzle game developed by Shady Paw and published by UMBC Game Developers Club, has just received a monumental 2.0 update that quadruples its size and enhances gameplay significantlyall for free! Players will once again step into the paws of Shady the Ghost Cat, who must navigate a world filled with challenges using his unique ability to become a shadow. The latest update not only expands the game but also introduces a plethora of new features that elevate the gaming experience.

With the addition of 24 new levels, multi-screen support, and new gameplay mechanics like conveyor belts and pressure pads, Phantom Feline is now more engaging than ever. The game is also Steam Deckverified and compatible with Linux, making it accessible to a wider audience. Plus, with the introduction of new collectibles and achievements, players have even more reasons to dive back into this delightful puzzle adventure.
