If you're a fan of puzzle games but often find them too challenging, then Botany Manor might just be the perfect fit for you. Currently available for free on the Epic Games Store, this cozy first-person puzzle game invites players to step into the shoes of botanist Arabella Greene as she explores a Victorian manor to complete her botanical research. Unlike other notorious puzzle games that can leave players feeling frustrated, Botany Manor strikes a balance between engaging challenges and a relaxing experience.

The developers have crafted a game that emphasizes exploration and discovery without overwhelming players with difficulty. Lead developer Laure De May highlighted the intention behind the game: to create a peaceful setting where the puzzles are thoughtfully designed to be engaging yet accessible. This makes Botany Manor an excellent choice for casual gamers or anyone looking to unwind while still enjoying a bit of mental stimulation.

As the gaming landscape continues to evolve, titles like Botany Manor remind us that not every game needs to be a brain-buster to be enjoyable. If you're looking for a game that allows you to relax and immerse yourself in a beautiful environment while solving puzzles, give Botany Manor a try while it's free. Will you take the plunge into this charming world of botany and puzzles?
