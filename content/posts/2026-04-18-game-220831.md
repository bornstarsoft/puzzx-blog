PEARL ABYSS has made headlines with its latest open-world action-adventure title, 'Crimson Desert', achieving a remarkable milestone of over 5 million copies sold globally within just 26 days of its launch. This achievement not only highlights the game's popularity but also marks a historic moment as it becomes the first title in Korean console game history to reach such a sales figure so quickly.

The company's announcement on April 15 (KST) expressed gratitude to the community of players, affectionately referred to as 'Greymanes', who have explored the vast world of Pywel and contributed to the game's success. For context, this sales pace outstrips that of other popular titles, such as 'Clair Obscur: Expedition 33', which took five months to reach the same benchmark, showcasing the growing appetite for innovative gaming experiences.
