Kodansha Game Lab and Octorain have unveiled TrailRail, an innovative 2D action puzzle game that promises to captivate players with its unique mechanics and engaging storyline. Set to launch on PC via Steam, this game introduces a novel concept where your movement trail becomes the very path that shapes the world around you, offering a fresh twist on puzzle-solving gameplay.

In TrailRail, players control Rail, a girl on a quest to rediscover her lost memories. With simple controls limited to movement, jumping, and materializing trajectories, the game challenges players to solve 38 intricately designed puzzles. The key to success lies in your intuition and creativityturn your flight path into bridges or footholds, and utilize physics-based actions to navigate through each stage. As you progress, youll uncover the mysteries of Rails origins and the world she inhabits.

With its blend of simplicity and depth, TrailRail is set to be a standout title for puzzle enthusiasts. Will you be ready to embark on this journey of discovery and creativity?
