In a bold move that merges two iconic gaming genres, the upcoming Steam title "Blocks for Babies" combines the frantic first-person shooter mechanics of DOOM with the classic puzzle gameplay of Tetris. Developed by indie studio BunkSoft Interactive, this game aims to evoke nostalgia while offering a fresh twist on familiar concepts. Players will not only stack blocks but also blast them away with a shotgun, creating a unique gaming experience that stands out in the crowded indie game market.

The gameplay shifts seamlessly from a cozy Tetris-style puzzle to a chaotic shooter, allowing players to earn power-ups and unlock new skills as they progress.
