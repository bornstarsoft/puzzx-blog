Kojima Productions has taken a bold step into the gaming hardware market with the announcement of the ROG Flow Z13-KJP, a 2-in-1 gaming tablet designed by renowned artist Yoji Shinkawa. This collaboration with ASUS Republic of Gamers (ROG) not only showcases cutting-edge technology but also infuses the essence of the beloved Ludens mascot into its design, making it a must-have for fans and gamers alike.

The ROG Flow Z13-KJP boasts impressive specifications, including a stunning 13.4-inch display with 2.5K resolution and a 180Hz refresh rate, powered by an AMD Ryzen AI Max+ 395 processor and Radeon 8060S Graphics. Alongside the tablet, a range of themed accessories such as the ROG Delta II-KJP gaming headset and ROG Keris II Origin-KJP Edition gaming mouse will further enhance the gaming experience. While pricing and release dates remain under wraps, the anticipation is palpable as these products are expected to launch in the first half of the year.
